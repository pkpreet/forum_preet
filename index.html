Vission and Values

Since 1974, we are in wood industries and serving quality woods in various divisions. We are continuously working to aim for excellence in this field. We were born in 2016 to serve you in the same glorious manner, as we did Since 1974 in wood sector. Now we are dealing with Plywood, Block Board , Door & Flexi ply . Our main motto is to serve our Honorable Customers with good Quality of our every product. We are very much committed to the quality of our every product . We strive to procure all our products through constant innovation and strict vigilance for maintaining the high standard of quality. To bring out the best of quality, we monitor our procurement process at every single step, thus providing supreme quality of the products in the market, so that every Rupee of our customers is worthwhile.
